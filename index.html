About the event ×
hackNAU is the first major computer science related event at North American University run by the NAU-ACM Chapter. Students from all over Houston will meet up at hackNAU to build the coolest and most innovative projects, meet amazing people, and win some prizes along the way! Attendance is only for college/university level students and is free of charge (food & admission) for all registered hackers.
